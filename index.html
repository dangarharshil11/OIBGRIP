There are many different types of Indian cuisines, from the North to the South . Each region has its own unique style of cooking, but they have one thing in common - great flavor! And you will find all kind of Different Types of Indian Food in Desi Pakwan

Pakwan is owned by two Gujrati brothers, Harshil and Ankit, who moved to the Ahmedabad to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. To craft the menu, Harshil relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef. Ankit does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Gujrati to incorporate additional cuisines from all of the india.
